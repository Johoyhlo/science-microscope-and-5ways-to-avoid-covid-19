Parts and uses of a MICROSCOPE

Eyepiece- Is an part of a microscope that let you see on the glass slide.

Body Tube- Part that connects to the objective/eye piece.

Nosepiece- Supports and connects to the lenses.

Arm- Supports the parts and body of the microscope.

Objective Lens- part that allows you to see small object.

Stage clip- Holds the glass slide and it supports.

Stage- Supports the stage clip and supports the body of the microscope.

Iris diaphragm- It adjust the light of the microscope to see.

Condenser lens- A part that adjust the light of the micoscope when the lens are not clear.

Light source- Lights up when the lens is not clear

course Focus- Moves the stage

Fine focus- Moves the stage of thhe microscope.

Stage Control Knob- moves the stage to make it clear

Base- Supports the whole body of the microscope and parts.
